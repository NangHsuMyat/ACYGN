Inform * Innovate * Inspire

American Center Yangon (ACY) is a place where you can learn about the United States,
explore new technologies, enhance your English language skills, and participate in
open dialogue on many topics.

Admission is free! Admission is free! American Center Yangon is open to the public,
Wednesday through Friday from 12:00 pm to 6:00 pm, and 10:00 am to 6:00 pm on
Saturdays, with extended hours for special occasions and events. The Center is also
closed on U.S. and some Myanmar holidays.

Become a member! As an ACY member, you will receive a membership card that
entitles you to wi-fi, and open computer lab access. You can borrow books and DVDs,
use tablets, and access online research databases. A one-year membership is currently
only 5,000 kyat and is open to all persons 16 years of age or older.
Activities and resources include:

  • Internet: Available any time for ACY members and to the general public during
    workshops and special events.


  • Library resources: The Library collection numbers over 12,000 books, magazines,
    DVDs (documentaries and commercial films), music CDs, and digital resources.
    The Library manages a Book Locker program to lend small collections to other
    libraries, community centers, and educational institutions in the country.


  • English language courses: Temporarily Closed.


  • EducationUSA: Free advising services are available to students interested
    in study in the United States – through appointments and regular information
    sessions. For more information, check: Facebook.com/educationusaburma.

  • Youth Clubs: The Center hosts interest groups and clubs. Follow us on Facebook
    or stop by the information counter for information on joining a club.


  • Special Events:
    • Tech Talks, MakerCorner, Youth Forum, U.S. speakers,
    cultural events, and more!
